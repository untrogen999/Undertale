Flowey

Character Info

Also Known As:

  • FLOWERY (Papyrus)

Origin

He is the first major character that the protagonist encounters in Undertale.

Flowey serves as the main antagonist for the majority of the game, especially in the Neutral and True Pacifist routes,

and can be considered a secondary main character for the Genocide Route. He provides an introduction

to the mechanics by the "friendliness pellets," which are actually harmful bullets,

revealing his philosophy of "KILL or BE KILLED".

Appearance

Flowey has the appearance of a golden flower. He

has a white center, yellow petals, and a light-green stem.

Flowey can change his face to show various expressions and can mimic the faces and voices

of other characters. He mimics Toriel and Asgore in some situations, as well as his

old self at the end of the Genocide Route. He also mimics the protagonist's face

before the Neutral Route's final battle.

Personality

When protagonist first meets Flowey, he hides under a facade of friendliness and politeness.

He often greets the you with southern slang, such as "Howdy!", calls himself "your best friend,"

and he uses polite words such as "gosh" and "golly" to the ends of his sentences.

Whenever he drops his disguised friendliness, he often calls the protagonist an "idiot."

Flowey is malevolent and cruel, yelling the protagonist for not following his "kill or be killed" philosophy.

He also criticizes the protagonist at the end of the Ruins if they killed any monster.

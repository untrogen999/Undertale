Asgore

Character Info

Also Known As:

  • King Fluffybuns
  • KING FLUFFYBOY
  • Mr.Dreamy
  • Gorey
  • ASGORE

Origin

Asgore Dreemurr is the ruler of the Underground and its denizens.

He is the ex-husband of Toriel, father of Asriel Dreemurr, adoptive father of

the first human. While not evil or malicious, he is one of the main

antagonists, planning to kill seven humans and use their SOULs to break the

barrier which traps the monsters in the Underground.

Appearance

Asgore is a Boss Monster with broad shoulders, a golden blond

beard and two large curved horns. He wears a suit of armor, a purple cape

and a crown on his head. The weapon he uses in battle is a long, red trident with a flower.

Asgore is shown to be the biggest main character in the entire game.

Towards the end of the game, Asgore looks more fat rather than broad-chested.

Personality

Asgore is a kind monster that enjoys gardening, Golden Flower Tea, and

Toriel's Butterscotch Pie. Like his son, he speaks in southern slang.

Before his battle, he offers the protagonist many opportunities to explore

the Underground and is reluctant to fight them at all; he even suggests that Toriel,

the protagonist and himself live as a family if the protagonist spares him

Asgore lives in the past and wants nothing more than to see his wife and child

again, even though they have been gone for a long time

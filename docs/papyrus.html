Papyrus

Character Info

Also Known As:

  • THE GREAT PAPYRUS (Papyrus)
  • Brother (Sans)

Origin

Papyrus is the brother of Sans and a major character.

His main motive is to capture a human so he can become a member of

the Royal Guard, and finally have friends and popularity.

He is one of two of the Skelebros.

Appearance

He is a tall,with a skull more vertical and geometric than his brother.

Being a skeleton, his expressions incorporate a toothy grin.

His eyes resemble vertical slits; his skeletal eyebrows form most of his expressions.

He has eyeballs which show at times when he is experiencing strong emotions, making his character

a form of comecial relief throughout the game.

Personality

Papyrus is a flamboyant skeleton who presents a confident, charismatic image of

himself. He works hard and has kind heart. He is optimistic and innocent.

He is also oblivious and nonobservant, but can be cunning at times

Papyrus believes in others just as much as he believes in himself.

He cooks spaghetti in his spare time, though many characters seem to

think that he is not very good at it.

Papyrus also watches Mettaton's TV shows and admires Mettaton.

Alphys

Character Info

Relationships:

  • Undyne (Romantic)
  • Asgore (Boss/Previous Love Interest)
  • Mettaton (Creation)
  • Flowey (Creation)

Origin

Dr. Alphys is a female, reptilian monster that lives in

the lab in western Hotland. She is the incumbent Royal Scientist, a position she

was awarded by Asgore after the disappearance of her predecessor.

Appearance

Alphys is a yellow, reptilian monster. Her posture is somewhat hunched over.

She has yellow scales, claws, and protruding teeth. Her feet have three toes

while there's three fingers and a thumb on each of her hands.

Her "hair" is designed into points facing the back of her head.

She wears round glasses a white lab coat. She wears a polka dot dress

on her date with the protagonist.

Personality

Alphys is a nerdy, shy recluse with a fondness for anime and manga.

She has crushes on multiple characters, but struggles to communicate her feelings.

Alphys is filled with guilt and insecurities about herself and her accomplishments.

After she accidentally created the Amalgamates while experimenting with determination,

she told nobody and became deeply depressed.

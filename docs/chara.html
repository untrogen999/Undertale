Chara

Character Info

Also Known As:

  • The First Human
  • The Fallen Human

Origin

Chara, also known as the first human or the fallen human, is the

first human to fall into the Underground. Chara is also the fallen human that

the player names at the start of the game, and not the controllable character

who is played throughout the entirety of Undertale

Appearance

Chara looks strikingly similar to the protagonist and just like the protagonist, has an

ambiguous gender. Both appear to have been around the same age after falling

into the Underground. Chara's SOUL is also the same color as the protagonist's,

At the end of the Genocide Route, Chara is seen to be wearing a lime and cream colored

striped shirt and red-brown pants and shoes.

Chara has peach skin and brown hair, rosy cheeks, open eyes, and a smile that

contrasts the protagonist's stoic expression.

Personality

Initially said to be sympathetic light, Asriel admits at the end of the True

Pacifist Route that Chara "...wasn't really the greatest person." Asriel says

that Chara climbed Mount Ebott for an unhappy reason, and adds that Chara "hated

humanity". At the end of the Genocide Route, Chara behaves in a polite and thanks

the protagonist for teaching them that power was the reason they awoke.

if the protagonist refuses to destroy the world, Chara becomes frustrated and

confused, wonders what the player wants instead, and then finally gives in to

the frustration, erasing the world anyway.
